Rock, Paper, Scissors

Invoke rps(item1, item2); in the console, where the arguments are either 'rock', 'paper', or 'scissors'. If some arguments are missing, they will be randomly generated for you.

The rps(..) function returns -1, 0, or 1 like any standard comparison function in most languages, where -1 or 1 means item1 or item2 won accordingly, or 0 for a tie.
